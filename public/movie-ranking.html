The Best Movies According to Rashi

My top 3 movies of all-time.

3 Idiots

It is a movie about engineering students, beautiful representation of friendships, hostel life, love and drama. It is is both funny and emotional

Yeh Jawani Hai Deewani

It is a movie in which a group of friends go to a trip together to Manali. It is about adult friendships. Lighthearted film to watch

Om Shanti Om

It is one of the first films I have watched, thus it holds a special place in my heart.
